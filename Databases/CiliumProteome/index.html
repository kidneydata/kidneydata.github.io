Primary Cilium Proteome Database

This database shows proteins identified in primary cilia of mouse IMCD3 cells, compiled from three different proteomic publications:

1. Biochemical isolation using calcium-shock. Ishikawa, H., Thompson, J., Yates III, J. R., & Marshall, W. F. (2012). Proteomic Analysis of Mammalian Primary Cilia. Current Biology, 22, 414-419. PMID: 22326026
2. Proximity labeling using cilia-APEX (NPHP3-APEX). Mick, D. U., Rodrigues, R. B., Leib, R. D., Adams, C. M., Chien, A. S., Gyri, S. P., Nachury, M. V. (2015). Proteomics of Primary Cilia by Proximity Labeling. Developmental Cell, 35(4), 497-512. PMID: 26585297
3. Proximity labeling using cilia-APEX2 (NPHP3-APEX2). May EA, Kalocsay M, D'Auriac IG, Schuster PS, Gygi SP, Nachury MV, Mick DU. Time-resolved proteomics profiling of the ciliary Hedgehog response. J Cell Biol. 2021;220(5):e202007207. PMID: 33856408

The full mouse IMCD3 transcriptome is viewable here. Database was created by Yash Mehta, Spencer Lewis, Kirby Leo, Vishwanathan Raghuram and Mark Knepper in the Epithelial Systems Biology Laboratory (Mark Knepper, Chief) at the National Heart, Lung and Blood Institute as part of its Kidney Systems Biology Project. The protein descriptor information was compiled using the Automated Bioinformatics Extractor (ABE). Click to download data as spreadsheet. Please cite: Mehta et al., 2022. PMID:34757121.
