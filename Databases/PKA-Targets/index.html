PKA Substrates

Phosphorylation Targets of Protein Kinase A (PKA).   This list includes all basophilic phosphorylation sites found to exhibit a decrease  in phospho-occupancy after CRISPR-Cas9 deletion of both catalytic subunits of PKA (Prkaca and Prkacb) in vasopressin-responsive mouse epithelial cells (mpkCCD). Phospho-occupancy was quantified in PKA double knockout (dKO) vs control (ctrl) cells by LC-MS/MS using SILAC method. Data represent a mean of three biological replicates, i.e. different PKA dKO clones and their respective control lines.   Cells were grown in the presence of the V2 receptor-selective vasopressin analog dDAVP (0.1 nM). Site created by Kiyoshi Isobe, Hyun Jun Jung, V. Raghuram, Chin-Rang Yang, Jevin Z. Clark and Mark Knepper of the Division of Intramural Research, NHLBI, Bethesda, Maryland.

Reference: Isobe K, Jung HJ, Yang CR, Claxton J, Sandoval P, Burg MB, Raghuram V, Knepper MA. Systems-level identification of PKA-dependent signaling in epithelial cells. Proc Natl Acad Sci U S A. 2017 Oct PMID: 28973931
