Database of Carbamylated Proteins in the Renal Inner Medulla

      This dataset contains all the carbamylated sites found in renal medullas of untreated rats by LC-MS/MS in the publication “Endogenous Carbamylation of Renal Medullary Proteins” by Claxton et. al. Using the spectral search algorithm InsPecT, we identified carbamylated peptides by the presence of a +43.005814 Da modification. The carbamyylation sites are represented by the single letter code of the amino acid and its position within the protein relative to the sequence contained in the Ref Seq record indicated. The full sequence of the peptides can be downloaded at: CarbamylationDatabase.xlsx
