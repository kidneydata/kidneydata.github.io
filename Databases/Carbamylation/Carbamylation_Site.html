Carbamylated Proteins in the Renal Inner Medulla Determined by LC-MS/MS

      This dataset contains all carbamylated sites, identified by LC-MS/MS, in proteins isolated from renal medullas of untreated rats in “Endogenous Carbamylation of Renal Medullary Proteins” by Claxton et. al. Using the spectral search algorithm InsPecT, we identified carbamylated peptides by the presence of a +43.005814 Da modification. The spectra can be observed by clicking on the peptide sequence (Matched ions are denoted by a dashed vertical line. The solid horizontal line in the sequence indicates that an amino acid exactly matches the theoretical mass). All spectra were manually examined to confirm the site assignment ('confident' vs. 'ambiguous'). Some peptides mapped to more than one protein (* in 'Protein Name'). Download data here.
