Database of Ubiquitylated Proteins in the Renal Inner Medulla

This dataset contains all the ubiquitylated sites found in renal medullas of untreated rats by LC-MS/MS in the publication “Endogenous Carbamylation of Renal Medullary Proteins” by Claxton et. al. Using the spectral search algorithm InsPecT, we identified ubiquitylated peptides by the presence of a +118.068034 Da modification. The ubiquitylation sites are represented by the single letter code of the amino acid and its position within the protein relative to the sequence contained in the Ref Seq record indicated. The full sequence of the peptides can be downloaded at: UbiquitylationDatabase.xlsx

Please cite: Claxton JS et al. Endogenous carbamylation of renal medullary proteins. PLoS One. 2013; 8: e82655. PMID:24386107.
