Rat Inner Medullary Collecting Duct (IMCD) Membrane Proteins

This protein list has been extracted from the full IMCD proteome found on the IMCD Proteome Database. The list includes both integral-membrane proteins and GPI-linked proteins. The list was reported in Yu et al., 2008, PMID: 18596208. All data are from IMCD cells freshly purified from rat inner medullas. See ESBL Databases for more extensive compendia of IMCD proteins.
