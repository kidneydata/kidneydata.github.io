Solute Carrier (SLC) Protein Expression along the Renal Tubule

__________________________________________________________________

This database provides protein expression levels for all Solute Carrier Proteins in each of 14 renal tubule segments from mouse kidney (Limbutara et al. PMID 32358040). Genome wide data can be found at proteomic data. Tubule segments: PTS1, S1 proximal tubule; PTS2, S2 proximal tubule; PTS3, S3 proximal tubule; DTL1, type 1 descending thin limb of Henle; DTL2, type 2 descending thin limb; DTL3, type 3 descending thin limb; ATL, ascending thin limb; MTAL, medullary thick ascending limb; CTAL, cortical thick ascending limb; DCT, distal convoluted tubule; CNT, connecting tubule; CCD, cortical collecting duct; OMCD, outer medullary collecting duct; IMCD, inner medullary collecting duct.

Reference: Lewis S et al. SLC-omics of the kidney: Solute transporters along the nephron. Am J Physiol Cell Physiol . 2021 PMID: 34191628.
