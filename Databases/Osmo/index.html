Database of Osmoregulated Proteins in Mammalian Cells

This database of osmoregulated proteins in mammalian cells is based on previously published high-throughput and hypothesis-driven data sets from the NIH NHLBI Systems Biology Center and other studies available in the literature. The database was created by Cameron R. Grady, Joan D. Ferraris, Maurice B. Burg, and Mark A. Knepper. "Hypertonicity" includes elevation of extracellular tonicity by the addition of inorganic ions such as NaCl or organic solutes such as sorbitol or raffinose. Email to: knepperm@nhlbi.nih.gov.

The data in the first five columns can be read as a sentence by either humans or computers. Additional columns contain ancillary information that can be viewed as a continuation of the sentence. This structure is used to facilitate computational network building. Click Here for Terminology
