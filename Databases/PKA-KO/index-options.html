Effect of Protein Kinase A Deletion on Transcriptome of mpkCCD Cells (RNA-Seq)

Brief Method: RNA-Seq was carried out in PKA double knockout mpkCCD cells (both PKA catalytic genes deleted) compared with mpkCCD lines with intact PKA expression.

Reference: Isobe K, Jung HJ, Yang CR, Claxton J, Sandoval P, Burg MB, Raghuram V, Knepper MA. Systems-level identification of PKA-dependent signaling in epithelial cells. Proc Natl Acad Sci U S A. 2017 Oct, PMID: 28973931
