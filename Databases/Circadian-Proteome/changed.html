Proteins Expressed at Significantly Different Levels between ZT12 and ZT0 in Mouse Kidney

Method: Whole right kidney samples from mice were obtained at ZT12 (n=3) and ZT0 (n=3) and processed for quantitative protein mass spectrometry using tandem mass tagging (TMT) for quantification. ZT12 is Zeitgeber Time 12 (time at lights off in 12:12 hour scheme). ZT0 is Zeitgeber Time 0 (time at lights on in 12:12 hour scheme). Log2 (ZT12/ZT0) is the base 2 logarithm of the protein abundance ratio between ZT12 and ZT0. P-value is from t-statistic of log2(protein abundance) values from the 6 samples. This page reports only differences with Pjoint less than 0.0005 (see "Download Data" for calculation). LC-MS/MS was carried out in an Orbitrap Fusion Lumos mass spectrometer (Thermo Scientific).
