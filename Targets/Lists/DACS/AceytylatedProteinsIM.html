Database of Acetylated Proteins in the Renal Inner Medulla

      This dataset contains all the acetylated sites found in renal medullas of untreated rats by LC-MS/MS in the publication “Endogenous Carbamylation of Renal Medullary Proteins” by Claxton et. al. Using the spectral search algorithm InsPecT, we identified acetylated peptides by the presence of a +42.010565 Da modification. The acetylation sites are represented by the single letter code of the amino acid and its position within the protein relative to the sequence contained in the Ref Seq record indicated. The full sequence of the peptides can be downloaded at: AcetylationDatabase.xlsx

Reference: Claxton JS, Sandoval PC, Liu G, Chou CL, Hoffert JD, Knepper MA. Endogenous carbamylation of renal medullary proteins. PLoS One. 2013; 8:e82655.PubMed PMID: 24386107
