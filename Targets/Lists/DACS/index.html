List of Lysine Deacetylases in Mammals


Below are the lysine deacetylases found in mammals. The information was culled and curated from several existing resources (click to open): Histome: The Histone Infobase, UniProtKB, NCBI, Rat Genome Database,Drazic et al.2016. By BLAST searches with conserved catalytic domains, we found 3 putative novel lysine deacetylase enzymes (Fam118A, Famm118B, HDA1L). HDAC1L is exclusively expressed by rats.

To see expression of the deacetylases along the rat nephron click here.
