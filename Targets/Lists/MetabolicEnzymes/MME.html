This is a database mapping metabolic enzymes to gene symbols and biological pathways. The data are available for download below.

Download data as an Excel file

Metabolic enzymes and pathways were extracted from Sigma-Aldrich and Roche metabolic charts, available online. Using data from ExPASy and PubMed regarding EC numbers, enzymes from the charts were mapped to genes, ultimately connecting metabolic pathway terms to official gene symbols. In addition to using information derived from the enzymatic pathway charts, gene ontology (GO) terms from NIAID's Database for Annotation, Visualization, and Integrated Discovery (DAVID) and the ESBL's Automated Bioinformatics Extractor (ABE) were found for each gene.

The data are presented here in a scrolling table format. To sort the data by a property of interest (for example, by "Gene Symbol"), click on the corresponding header. To sort in reverse order, simply click again. Gene symbols are linked to their associated pages in PubMed. The gene symbols and annotations correspond to Rattus norvegicus genes but are common to most mammals, to include mice and humans.
