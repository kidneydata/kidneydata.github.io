Database of Metabolic Enzymes Expressed in Kidney Tubule Segments. This searchable, sortable database lists official gene symbols for mammalian metabolic enzymes and their expression levels in different segments of the rat kidney, ascertained from transcriptomic data (Affymetrix expression arrays). The transcriptomic data were from two papers: Uawithya et al., Physiol Genomics, 2008 and Yu et al., Proc Natl Acad Sci USA, 2009. See the ESBL's Mammalian Metabolic Enzyme Database for full annotation.

Download full dataset as a spreadsheet

Transcriptomic data are based on Affymetrix microarray profiling carried out by the ESBL on cells isolated from rat kidneys. Signal intensities have been normalized such that the median value is 1.0, and transcripts with signal intensity above background (0.4) are considered to be expressed. All renal transcriptomic data are also available via the Transcriptomes of Major Nephron Segments page. Data may be filtered by Roche sectors using the drop-down filter at right. Abbreviations: PT, Proximal Tubule; MTAL, Medullary Thick Ascending Limb of Henle; IMCD, Inner Medullary Collecting Duct.
