Serine/Threonine Protein Phosphatase Catalytic Subunits

This database lists all Serine/Threonine Phosphatase Catalytic Subunits in the mouse genome. It was derived from the DEPOD Database and converted to mouse annotations using the Automated Bioinformatics Extractor (ABE). A related database of S/T Phosphatase Regulatory Subunits is available at the following link.

Database was created by Sophia LeMaire and Mark Knepper in the Epithelial Systems Biology Laboratory (Mark Knepper, Chief) at the National Heart, Lung and Blood Institute as part of its Kidney Systems Biology Project. Reference: LeMaire SM, Raghuram V, Grady CR, Pickering CM, Chou CL, Umejiego EN, Knepper MA. Serine/threonine phosphatases and aquaporin-2 regulation in renal collecting duct. Am J Physiol Renal Physiol. 2017 Jan 1;312(1):F84-F95. PMID: 27784696
